The World of "Fairy Tail 100 Year Quest"

"Fairy Tail 100 Year Quest" serves as a direct continuation of the original "Fairy Tail" manga created by Hiro Mashima. The story takes place after the defeat of the main antagonist Zeref and the disbandment of the Fairy Tail guild. To further challenge the guild's strength and reunite the scattered members, the Fairy Tail members embark on a daring quest known as the "100 Year Quest."

2. New Challenges and Adventures

The quest itself is a monumental undertaking, as it involves completing a mission that was deemed impossible for over a century. The guild members, including Natsu Dragneel, Lucy Heartfilia, Gray Fullbuster, and Erza Scarlet, face formidable adversaries, ancient mysteries, and powerful magic as they strive to overcome the challenges that await them. The "100 Year Quest" pushes the limits of their abilities and tests the bonds of friendship that make Fairy Tail so resilient.
